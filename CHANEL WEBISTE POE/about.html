ABOUT CHANEL

Elegance, innovation, and timeless style since 1910

OUR STORY

Founded in 1910 by Gabrielle "Coco" Chanel in Paris, Chanel revolutionized women's fashion with timeless elegance, simplicity, and innovation. Coco Chanel liberated women from the constraints of the corseted silhouette and popularized a sporty, casual chic as the feminine standard of style.

Today, under the creative direction of Virginie Viard, Chanel continues to lead in luxury fashion, accessories, fragrances, and beauty while staying true to the founding principles of elegance, quality, and innovation.

The iconic Chanel No. 5 perfume, the little black dress, the Chanel suit, and the 2.55 quilted leather handbag remain timeless symbols of the brand's enduring legacy.
